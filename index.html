Data Products: Power and Challenges

If data is the new oil. Data Science is the new refinery. True value of data can only be captured if it is collected, properly pipelined, cleaned and curated and then presented in a way to make business sense leading to better decision making. As a data scientist, I believe in building data products which enable complex decision making. Good data products are tools which allow business owners with little experience in data analysis to connect business understanding with their data.
